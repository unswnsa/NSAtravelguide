How it works

A living guide that grows
with our community

Members submit tips via Notion, we approve them, and they appear here automatically — with photos and an interactive map.

📝
01

Submit your tip

Fill in our form with the place name, city, category, your description, up to 3 photo URLs, and a Google Maps link.

✅
02

We review it

Our team checks each entry in Notion and marks it as Approved. Usually within a day or two.

🗾
03

It goes live here

A GitHub Action syncs approved entries every hour. Your photos and map pin appear on this page automatically.
